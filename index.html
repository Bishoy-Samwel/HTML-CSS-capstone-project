Chef World Summit

The Chefs World Summit is an innovative and targeted concept, with a unique format!

The Chefs World Summit is an innovative concept, with a unique format: 3 days of conferences and interactive workshops will be led by the greatest Chefs and experts in the field - 5500sqm dedicated to exceptional products.

The Chefs World Summit brings together Chefs and culinary professionals from all over the world in this magical place where gastronomy rhymes with art of living.

A unique meeting to write the Gastronomy of tomorrow with Chefs and other Experts all ready to share and transmit their experience.

Conferences, debates and practical workshops address many interesting themes, aimed at all profiles of the catering industry.

A true global hub for the culinary arts, this event is designed to train and inspire the new generation of chefs, pastry chefs, restaurateurs, sommeliers, maîtres d'hôtel, sous-chefs, caterers, chocolatiers, teachers and students from cooking and hotel schools and many more.
